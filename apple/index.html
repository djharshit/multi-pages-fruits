Apple

The apple is a pome (fleshy) fruit that comes from the apple tree. Apples are one of the most widely cultivated tree fruits, and they are grown in temperate climates across the world. There are over 7,500 known cultivars of apples that range in flavor, size, color, and texture.

Apples can be eaten fresh off the tree or used in a variety of cooked dishes. They are high in fiber and vitamin C. Some of the most popular apple varieties include Red Delicious, Granny Smith, Fuji, Gala, Golden Delicious, and McIntosh.
